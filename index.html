Embedded Systems Design

Hardware built
from the ground up.

Logic One SA designs custom embedded systems for clients who need hardware that doesn't exist off the shelf. Whether you're building an IoT device, an industrial controller, or a smart product — we take your requirements from concept through to a working, testable prototype.

We work with a range of microcontroller platforms including STM32, ESP32, Arduino and Raspberry Pi, and design around your constraints — power budget, form factor, connectivity and cost.

Every project includes full firmware development, hardware testing and documentation — so what we hand over is ready to manufacture or iterate.

C-01

Microcontroller Design

STM32, ESP32, AVR, ARM Cortex-M — architecture selection and schematic design.

C-02

PCB Layout & Prototyping

Single and multi-layer PCB design, Gerber output, and prototype build and test.

C-03

Firmware Development

Bare-metal C/C++ and RTOS firmware. Sensor drivers, communication stacks, power management.

C-04

IoT & Wireless Integration

Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Zigbee, LoRa and MQTT — cloud connectivity and local network integration.

C-05

Sensor & Actuator Integration

IMUs, environmental sensors, motor drivers, relay boards — full peripheral integration.

C-06

Testing & Documentation

Functional testing, debugging, and full schematic/firmware documentation on every project.

About Logic One SA

Engineering-first.
Adelaide-based.

Logic One SA is an Adelaide electronics engineering business with a hands-on approach — we design custom embedded systems and repair precision devices, and we take both seriously.

Our embedded design work is built on real engineering depth — not off-the-shelf modules bodged together. And our repair work goes beyond part-swapping — we diagnose at the circuit level and know these platforms inside out.

Every project and repair comes with straight talk and honest pricing. As an authorised repair centre for both Roborock and Segway, we handle warranty and out-of-warranty work — with a clear inspection fee and firm quote before any repair begins. We're based in South Australia and work with local businesses, makers and individuals.
