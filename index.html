What we repair

Electronic Repairs.

Authorised repair centre for Roborock and Segway Ninebot — genuine parts, certified process, warranty and out-of-warranty service.

As an authorised repair centre, Logic One SA follows the official warranty processes for both Roborock and Segway Ninebot. Every repair uses genuine manufacturer parts and is carried out by technicians trained to manufacturer standards. Out-of-warranty repairs are accepted subject to an inspection fee assessment.

Genuine Parts

Manufacturer-sourced components only — no generic substitutes

Authorised Process

Official warranty procedures followed for both brands

Qualified Technicians

Brand-trained for Roborock and Segway Ninebot repairs

Adelaide Based

Local service — drop off or arrange freight from anywhere in SA

Out-of-warranty repairs are subject to an inspection fee. This covers diagnostic assessment and a repair quote — if you choose not to proceed, only the inspection fee applies.
